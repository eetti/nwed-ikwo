1

Jesus eyekan k’odudu

Kpukpru mme ebiet k’ison ;

Nsinsi Andiyana

Eyetie ke ubon.

Se owo eda nkpo do,

Eke mmo ekponode,

Eduok, enyun ese Obon,

Emi mmo etorode.

2

Ndien ndito Gentile kpukpru

Eyewara ndiyom,

K’edime esit, Enye,

Obon ye Abasi Ibom.

Ndien Israel eyeboho

K’ini Jesus Christ, Obon,

Andiyana mmo, edide

Ndikar’ ofuri ison.
