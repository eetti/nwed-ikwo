1

Abuat mmo ke okoneyo

K’oto oro k’inwang

Angel Obong oto k’enyong

Osuhore k’isong

Mbon enyong ekwo ikwo

Ikwo enem osong

Ekwo emem nti owo

K’iso ofuri isong.

2

Etat mfuong, esuk edi,

Esiak ikpa-enyong

Ikwo ekwode ososim

Mm’idiok owo k’isong

Ndien k’ufot afanikong

Y’enwan, k’otu owo

Y’ukut, y’ubiak, ye mmon-eyet.

Itreke ndikwo.

3

Edi mbon enyong k’ekwo

K’otu akwa oyom

Ndisi owo esuk enam

Mme idiok nkpo k’isong

Ndien oto k’uyom enwan

Owo ikopk’ nkpo

O ererimbot, kpang utong

Esuk ekwo ikwo.
