1

Yak mi nkpere Fi,

Abasi mi,

Cross okpobionode,

O nyana mi.

Ofuri ikwo ’yedi,

Yak mi nkpere Fi,

Nkpere Fi.

2

K’ini nsanade

K’okoneyo,

Nkwe nkpo aba

Nsuhore nna,

Ke ndap ami nyedi

Nkper’ Abasi mi,

Nkpere Fi.

3

O yak udoro-ukot

Osim enyon ;

Kpukpru mme eno Fo,

Ono ke mfon.

Mm’angel esuk ekot

Yak mi nkpere Fi,

Nkpere Fi.

4

K’ini ndemede,

Nnyun ndaha nda,

Nda itiat mmen

Beth-el k’enyon.

Afanikon mi mi

Eyenam nkpere Fi,

Nkpere Fi,

5

Ke mba ndok nsim

Ikpa-enyon,

Nkwe aba utin

Ye ofion ;

Ikwo mi eyedi

Yak mi nkpere Fi,

Nkpere Fi.
