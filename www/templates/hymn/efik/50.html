1

Hosanna, kwo hosanna,

Ke esit ye uyo ;

Nte mmo oro ekwode,

K’ini eyo oko.

Ekwo eno Ufan nnyin,

Kpa Jesus Christ Obon,

Emi amade kpukpru

Oruk owo k’ison.

2

Eto Obot Olive,

Akwa otu owo

Ke efefun edaak eyop,

K’ekwo ikwo nko.

Mme angel edian uyo mmo,

Ko ke edem enyon ;

Enyun ekwo hosanna,

Enyun eno ubon.

3

Ke ini mmo ekwode,

Eda ikon oro,

Kpan edisuan mmo k’usun

Obon ebede do.

Ntre Obon mme angel,

Ye Andibot owo,

Onyime se enamde,

Adat esit nko.

4

Hosanna, kwo hosanna,

Nnyin kpukpru iyekwo ;

Koro Christ akafak nnyin,

Anam idi ndito.

O yak isuk ikpono,

Edidem ye Obon,

Emi amade kpukpru

Oruk owo k’ison.
