1

Edara Jesus Christ Obon,

Obon ata uwem ;

Esio anyanya ubon

Eyara, ‘yara, ‘yara,

Christ nte Edidem.

2

Ndito Israel, emi

Enyenede item

Abasi okono owo,

Ekpono, ‘kpono, ‘kpono,

Christ nte Edidem.

3

Mme Gentile, mbufo edi

Kpukpru oruk usem ;

Edara Andinyana owo,

Ekpono, ‘kpono, ‘kpono,

Christ nte Edidem.

4

Yak mmo k’enyon, ye mmo k’ison,

Esana kiet ntem ;

K’akwa mkpo idaresit

Ekpono, ‘kpono, ‘kpono,

Christ nte Edidem.
