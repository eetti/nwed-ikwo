1

O come and let us sing

Unto the Lord, our God;

O Let us our Rock and Saviour sing

Who saves us all in love.

2

Let us before Him come

With thanksgiving and praise

And show ourselves with songs of joy

And sing unto His name.

3

The Lord is a great God;

A great king over all

His kingdom forever shall be

Without an end at all.

4

Within His are hands are all

The comers of the earth;

And all the lofty hills on earth

Are all within His hand.

5

The sea He also made

And it belongs to Him

And with His hand this earth He made

And thus be glorified.

6

O come, let us worship

And do bow down in pray’r

And kneel before the Lord, and King

Our maker and our friend.

7

For He’s the Lord our God;

And we shall be His flock

The flock He pastures, cares and loves

And sherpherds by Himself.

8

O hear the Lord His voice

O hear Him speaking know

And harden not your heart, for now

The patient time is gone
